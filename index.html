Our Mission

Reimagine Waste. Reignite Curiosity.

Building Tomorrow's Engineers

At ReCrafted Futures, we think that each and every piece of garbage can spark a new idea. "Reimagine Waste. Reignite Curiosity." isn't just our sloganâ€”it's our mission. We collect recyclable materials from neighborhood drives and recycle them into hands-on engineering sets for Title 1 schools. Those sets power imagination, instill problem-solving skills, and give kids the power to make something special out of something others might throw away. Join us by sponsoring a recycling drive or making a gift to engineering education, and assist in turning everyday materials into opportunities for discovery.
